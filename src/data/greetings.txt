Hidden Figures was an inspirational movie that highlighted three amazing African American women mathematicians.
Katherine Johnson, Dorothy Vaughan and Mary Jackson all were essential contributors to the National Aeronatucs and Space Administration Space Race.
To learn about these three phenomenal women or ask questions about them use the command !katherinejohnson !maryjackson and !dorothyvaughan. Example
!katherinejohnson How many papers did she write?
